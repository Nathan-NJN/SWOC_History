The History/Founders

The History of SWOC/Founders. One day, the Founder AKA
The Director was playing with a DOMS Stationery Scale when he flipped it upside down. To his surprise, the "DOMS Logo" became SWOC! Name that felt powerful and unique.

Realizing its potential, he embraced it as the identity of something new. Soon, the Co-Founder AKA the CEO, joined him, and together they built SWOC into more than just a name or a group chat, it became a unique Company and a brotherhood.

The SWOC Bois thrived under their leadership, which was filled with laughter, ideas, and memories. As their bond grew stronger, they created a Discord server, forming a digital clubhouse for their group. Later, they launched a Minecraft server, expanding SWOC into a virtual world where their friendship could thrive beyond just chats. What started as a simple discovery turned into a lasting legacy.
